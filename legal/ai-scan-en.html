AutoLog AI Data Notice

Version 1 · Effective: July 19, 2026 · autolog1.support@gmail.com

1. When this notice applies

This notice applies when you choose to use AI features in AutoLog (currently document recognition from a photo). AI features are optional; you can enter data manually. Cloud processing must not start until you accept the current notice.

2. What is sent

AutoLog provides a redaction tool. After you review and redact a document image, AutoLog sends the redacted/edited image itself to Google services (Firebase / Gemini) for multimodal field recognition. AI does not send OCR text instead of the image and does not run recognition only on your device. The unedited original is not intended to be sent to the AI service.

The request may include the document type, account and request identifiers, an app/device integrity token, subscription tier, and quota usage. It does not include your Google password or payment-card details.

3. Your responsibility

Before continuing, remove names, addresses, signatures, document numbers, payment details, faces, and any other information you do not want to disclose. You are responsible for personal data that remains visible. Do not submit another person's personal data without a lawful reason and permission.

4. Processing and storage

Google processes the edited image to return structured field suggestions. Processing and any service-side retention are governed by the applicable Google Privacy Policy and Firebase / Gemini terms. AutoLog does not use the image to train its own model and does not intentionally persist it in its own database.

Results are shown for your review and stored in AutoLog only if you save them. Firebase stores the user/account identifier, Pro or Business tier, monthly quota usage, request reservations, and abuse-prevention records needed to operate and secure AI features.

5. Your choices

  • Cancel and continue entering data manually.
  • Review every recognized field before saving.
  • Material changes increment the consent version and require acceptance again.
  • See the Privacy Policy for cloud records and deletion information.
  • Use of AI is also subject to the Terms of Use, including your duty to review AI-generated suggestions.
