Delete account and data — AutoLog

App: AutoLog (package com.tetesh.autolog) · AutoLog developer · autolog1.support@gmail.com

How to request account and related cloud data deletion

  1. Use the Google account email you signed in with for AutoLog cloud backup, or state in the email that you only used AI Scan without Google Sign-In.
  2. Email autolog1.support@gmail.com.
  3. Subject: AutoLog — account deletion.
  4. Include: the app name AutoLog, your Google email (if any), and a request to delete your Firebase account and related records (AI Scan quota, entitlement tier, reservations).
  5. We will confirm and delete cloud account data, typically within 30 days (often sooner).

What we delete

  • Your anonymous or linked Firebase Auth user identifier (AI Scan / linked profile).
  • Firebase records for Pro/Business tier, monthly AI Scan quota, scan reservations, and related abuse-prevention data.

What you should delete yourself

  • Local data (service log, expenses, photos, parking, etc.) — uninstall AutoLog.
  • Google Drive backups — delete the backup in AutoLog settings or in your Google Drive app folder.
  • .autolog export files you saved yourself — delete those files manually.
  • Google Play subscription — cancel in Google Play → Subscriptions; payments and purchase history are handled by Google, not the AutoLog developer.

What may be retained and for how long

  • Limited records may be kept longer when required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or Google Play billing records.
  • Data processed only by Google (Drive, Play Billing, Gemini) is governed by Google’s policies; AutoLog does not intentionally store AI Scan images in its own app database.

Partial data deletion without deleting the account

You can:

  • delete individual records and photos in the app;
  • delete the Drive cloud backup without permanently closing everything;
  • sign out of Google in AutoLog settings (this does not always erase Firebase records — use the account deletion steps above for a full cloud account wipe).

More details: AutoLog Privacy Policy.
