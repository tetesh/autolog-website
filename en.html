AutoLog

Car service book — offline, on your phone

AutoLog tracks maintenance, insurance, fuel, and expenses, sets reminders, and analyzes spending. Data stays on your device; backup to .autolog file or Google Drive. AI Scan processes documents on-device — nothing is sent to our servers for AI.

Core features

  • One car in the free version
  • Service, insurance & expense log
  • Date and mileage reminders
  • Optional add reminder to system calendar
  • Spending analytics
  • .autolog export & import
  • Russian & English

AutoLog Pro

  • Multiple cars
  • PDF reports
  • Cloud backup to Google Drive
  • Clean interface without ads
  • Parts catalog
  • CSV export
  • Parking — save GPS when you tap the button
  • Home screen widget: car, reminders, quick mileage update
  • AI Scan — photo of a receipt, policy, or invoice → draft record; on-device OCR & AI (experimental)
