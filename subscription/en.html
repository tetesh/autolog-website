Manage AutoLog subscription

Russia and Belarus · YooKassa

How billing works

In Russia and Belarus, AutoLog Pro/Business is billed via YooKassa (card or SBP), not Google Play. Yandex ID is used to sign in and restore the subscription (we store only the account id).

Current prices and plans are shown in the app before checkout (for example Pro: 99 ₽/month or 599 ₽/year). There is no YooKassa trial period.

How to cancel

  • Open AutoLog → Pro → Cancel subscription.
  • Confirm cancellation in the app.
  • Access continues until the end of the paid period; auto-renew turns off.

Auto-renewal (YooKassa)

A YooKassa subscription renews automatically at the end of each paid period unless you cancel beforehand. If a charge fails (insufficient funds, bank limits, etc.), the app shows a warning in the subscriptions section and sends a device notification; we retry the charge about once an hour for up to 3 days. Check your card or contact your bank.

Google Play

If you subscribed via Google Play (outside the YooKassa path), manage it in Google Play subscriptions. Failed renewals and payment reminders are handled by Google Play’s own notifications — not by AutoLog’s YooKassa warning.
