AutoLog Privacy Policy

Effective: July 17, 2026 · Contact: autolog1.support@gmail.com

1. Overview

AutoLog is an offline car service log. Core data stays on your device. The app has no backend servers.

2. Data on device

Stored locally: vehicles, maintenance, insurance, fuel, expenses, reminders, part catalog (Pro), saved parking spots — GPS coordinates when you tap “Save location” (Pro), photos, and settings. We do not access this data.

3. Backup

.autolog files — you export yourself. Google Drive (optional, Pro) — backup in your Drive app folder after sign-in. On devices without Google services, cloud backup is unavailable — use .autolog files.

4. Google Sign-In

The app receives your account email and Drive app-folder access. Governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

5. AI Scan (Pro, optional)

Document scanning runs on your device: you take a photo or pick an image; text is recognized with on-device OCR; on capable devices an on-device Qwen2.5 model (~1.6 GB) may be downloaded from Hugging Face / litert-community (not bundled in the APK). Field extraction stays local — document photos/text are not sent to the developer’s servers for AI. After extraction you review and save the record yourself. Downloading the model needs internet and may use substantial data. Model license: Apache License 2.0.

6. Location

We do not track location in the background. GPS is used only when you save a parking spot (Pro). Coordinates stay on your device and may be included in .autolog or Google Drive backups. We do not send coordinates to our servers.

7. What we do not do

  • We do not sell data
  • We do not use ad trackers
  • We do not collect contacts or microphone data
  • Camera is used only when you take a photo for a record, part catalog entry, or AI Scan
  • Calendar — the app may open the system calendar with a pre-filled event; it does not read your calendar data

8. Notifications

Delivered locally: service reminders (maintenance, insurance); an occasional update your mileage nudge (about every 7 days if mileage has not been updated recently). Notification data is not sent to the developer’s servers.

9. Pro payments

Processed by Google Play. Payment details are handled by the store, not by us.

10. Deletion

Uninstalling removes local data, including a downloaded AI model (if any). Google Drive backups remain until you delete them. Exported .autolog files remain wherever you save them.

11. Children

Not intended for children under 13.
