AutoLog Privacy Policy

Effective: July 6, 2026 · Contact: support@autolog.app

1. Overview

AutoLog is an offline car service log. Core data stays on your device. The app has no backend servers.

2. Data on device

Stored locally: vehicles, maintenance, insurance, fuel, expenses, reminders, photos, and settings. We do not access this data.

3. Backup

.autolog files — you export yourself. Google Drive (optional) — backup in your Drive app folder after sign-in.

4. Google Sign-In

The app receives your account email and Drive app-folder access. Governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

5. What we do not do

  • We do not sell data
  • We do not use ad trackers
  • We do not collect location or contacts
  • Camera is used only when you take a photo

6. Notifications

Reminders are delivered locally from your device.

7. Pro payments

Processed by RuStore or Google Play. Payment details are handled by the store.

8. Deletion

Uninstalling removes local data. Google Drive backups remain until you delete them.

9. Children

Not intended for children under 13.

10. Contact

support@autolog.app
