AutoLog Privacy Policy

Effective: July 19, 2026 · Contact: autolog1.support@gmail.com

1. Overview

AutoLog is an offline-first car service log with optional Google-powered cloud features. Core service-log data stays on your device. User/account identifiers, entitlement tier, AI Scan quota, reservation, and abuse-prevention records are stored in Firebase.

2. Data on device

Stored locally: vehicles, maintenance, insurance, fuel, expenses, reminders, part catalog (Pro), saved parking spots — GPS coordinates when you tap “Save location” (Pro), photos, and settings. We do not access this data.

3. Backup

.autolog files — you export yourself. Google Drive (optional, Pro) — backup in your Drive app folder after sign-in. On devices without Google services, cloud backup is unavailable — use .autolog files.

4. Google Sign-In

When you sign in for backup or AI Scan, the app receives your account email, a Firebase user identifier and, for backup, Drive app-folder access. Governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

5. AI Scan

AI Scan is an optional Pro/Business cloud feature. AutoLog provides a redaction tool. After versioned consent, the redacted/edited document image itself is sent to Google Firebase / Gemini for multimodal recognition. This is not an OCR-text-only or on-device AI process. The unedited source image is not intended to be sent to the AI recognition service.

Google returns suggested structured fields for your review. They are saved locally only if you save the record. AutoLog does not train its own model on the image and does not intentionally persist it in its own database. Google's processing and any service-side retention are governed by the applicable Google, Firebase, and Gemini terms.

Firebase stores the user/account identifier, Pro or Business tier, monthly quota usage, scan reservations, and abuse-prevention records. You are responsible for personal data left visible and for having a lawful basis to submit another person's data. See the AI Scan Data Notice.

The local ArticleScanner for selecting article numbers from labels is a separate, free on-device OCR tool; it does not use cloud AI Scan.

6. Location

We do not track location in the background. GPS is used only when you save a parking spot (Pro). Coordinates stay on your device and may be included in .autolog or Google Drive backups. We do not send coordinates to our servers.

7. What we do not do

  • We do not sell data
  • We do not use ad trackers
  • We do not collect contacts or microphone data
  • Camera is used only when you take a photo for a record, part catalog entry, or AI Scan
  • Calendar — the app may open the system calendar with a pre-filled event; it does not read your calendar data

8. Notifications

Delivered locally: service reminders (maintenance, insurance); an occasional update your mileage nudge (about every 7 days if mileage has not been updated recently). Notification data is not sent to the developer’s servers.

9. Pro and Business payments

Recurring Pro and Business subscriptions are processed by Google Play; there is no Lifetime tier. We receive purchase status, product/base-plan information, and a purchase token to verify the tier. Payment-card details are handled by Google, not by us. See the Terms of Use.

10. Deletion

Uninstalling removes local data. Google Drive backups remain until you delete them. Exported .autolog files remain wherever you save them. Signing out does not necessarily delete Firebase records. To request deletion of cloud records, email autolog1.support@gmail.com from the account concerned; limited records may be retained for security, billing, or legal requirements.

11. Children

Not intended for children under 13.
